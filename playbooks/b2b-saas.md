# SBL Playbook — B2B SaaS & Tech Founders

*211,000 delivered messages · 529 campaigns*

## Who this is for
Founders, VP Sales, RevOps leads, CROs selling B2B software. Targeting other founders, executives, or technical buyers. Company size 1–500 employees.

---

## Core insight
SaaS founders get 10–15 outreach messages per week. A mediocre message to someone who showed a behavioral signal beats excellent copy sent cold — every time. Build around signals, not volume.

---

## Campaign types (ranked by quality)

### 1. Community Signal Outreach
Target members of communities your ICP belongs to (YC alumni, RevGenius, SaaSRise, etc.)

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| CR acceptance | 35–51% |
| Reply rate | 8–15% |
| Hot leads per 100 delivered | 4–7 |
| Avg conversation depth | 3–5 messages |

**Lead sources:** LinkedIn company search for the community name → export members. Sales Navigator keyword filter: `"RevGenius" OR "SaaSRise" OR "YC W24"`.

**Setup pattern:**
- Sender: your own LinkedIn profile (community reference only lands if you're a real member)
- Message: soft nudge referencing the community — no pitch, goal is the connection
- Follow-up: voice note introducing what you do after they connect
- Chat flow: ENGAGE → IDENTIFY outreach situation → PITCH solution
- Smart FU: 1–2 max, warm check-in not a re-pitch

---

### 2. Post Engagement Targeting
Target people who liked or commented on a specific LinkedIn post relevant to your ICP.

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| CR acceptance | 30–44% |
| Reply rate | 12–20% |
| Positive sentiment | 65–75% |
| Hot leads per 100 delivered | 6–9 |
| Comment-to-DM conversion | 15–25% |

**Lead sources:**
- Post Likes: SBL → Sources → Leads via Post Likes → paste post URL
- Comment to DM: SBL → Sources → Comment to DM → paste post URL (warmest)
- Competitor posts: target people engaging with competitor content

**Good posts to target:** Posts by SaaS CEOs on AI replacing SDRs, scaling without headcount, signal-based prospecting.

**Setup pattern:**
- Sender: SDR profile or Fractional SDR account
- Message: reference the specific post, create curiosity, don't over-pitch
- Follow-ups: 2–3 voice notes (one per day, new angle each: social proof, use case, final ping)
- Chat flow: ENGAGE referencing post → IDENTIFY if they want to track signals → PITCH → give two routes (DIY trial / Guided setup)
- Smart FU: 3 conditions — went quiet after route choice, didn't sign up after link, Calendly link went dead

---

### 3. Sales Navigator + ICP Filter
Build a precise ICP in Sales Navigator, run ICP Filter before any message goes out.

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| CR acceptance | 13–20% |
| Reply rate | 6–10% |
| Hot leads per 100 delivered | 2–4 |
| Volume (10 profiles) | 2,000 connection requests/week |

**Sales Navigator search:**
```
Job Title:    Founder OR Co-Founder OR CEO OR "VP Sales" OR "Head of Sales" OR RevOps OR CRO
Company size: 11–50 OR 51–200
Industry:     Computer Software OR Internet OR SaaS
Geography:    [target market]
Seniority:    Director OR VP OR C-Suite OR Owner
Activity:     Posted on LinkedIn: Last 30 days  ← non-negotiable
```

**Setup pattern:**
- Sender: 5–10 Fractional SDR profiles, SBL auto-rotates
- Message: direct, concrete value prop under 250 characters — no community/post reference since cold
- A/B test: 2–3 message variations, kill underperformers after 200 delivers
- Follow-ups: 2 (first: few hours, voice note, warm and light; second: next day, different angle)
- Chat flow: ENGAGE → IDENTIFY specific bottleneck → PITCH → offer trial
- Smart FU: interest-based check-in, then timing-aware second

---

## Message guidelines
- Under 250 characters for cold openers
- No "scale your pipeline", "book more demos", "transform your outreach"
- Peer-to-peer tone — founder talking to founder
- Lead with behavioral signal reference when possible
- One specific outcome > three generic benefits

---

## Dashboard benchmarks to watch
| Signal | Healthy | Action needed |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| CR acceptance | 25%+ | < 15%: opener too salesy or wrong ICP |
| Reply rate | 8%+ | < 5%: cut message, add specificity |
| Friction (red) | < 10% | > 15%: remove pitch words from opener |
| Disengaged (yellow) | < 35% | > 40%: fix Smart Follow-up timing |

---

## Fixing what's not working
- **Low acceptance (< 15%):** Switch to community signal or post-reference opener. Tighten ICP Filter.
- **Low reply rate (< 5%):** Cut to under 250 chars. Remove generic phrases. A/B test versions.
- **High friction (> 15%):** Remove all pitch words from opener. Shift to Campaign 1 or 2 first.
- **High disengaged (> 40%):** First follow-up must be at 24h. Follow-up adds new angle, not re-pitch. Add voice note as final FU.

---

## Scaling what works
1. After 200–300 delivers, confirm winner
2. Kill underperforming versions
3. Add more Fractional SDR profiles to winning campaign
4. Duplicate winner → adjust opener slightly → run new variation
5. Every 2 weeks: review sentiment, adjust chat flow for top objections
